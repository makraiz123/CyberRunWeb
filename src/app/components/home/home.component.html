About Us


Cyber Run PH is an ambitious effort to cultivate a passionate community of runners within the country. It promotes proper fitness and healthy lifestyle by incorporating Running in everyday life. It aspires to motivate all Filipinos to run more frequently for a better lifestyle that we all owe to ourselves. It provides an avenue for beginner runners to be better at the sport and for more experienced runners to test themselves and improve their runs to be more efficient and fulfilling. It hopes to establish Metro Manila as a running capital where anyone and everyone can enjoy the sport in an encouraging and safe environment.
